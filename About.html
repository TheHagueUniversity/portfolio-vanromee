About me

About me

Hey there! I’m Romée van Fulpen, a UX Designer student based in Den Haag, the Netherlands.

I’m Dutch but have an Indonesian background. I was born in Utrecht, the Netherlands and lived there until the age of 12, when my family and I moved to Düsseldorf, Germany.

In 2018, I graduated with an IB diploma from the International School of Düsseldorf, where I have lived for 7 years. Growing up surrounded by international students helped me to easily adapt to new languages and cultures. One of my favourite experiences is going to Tanzania as part of a volunteering project. There I got to teach English and did arts and crafts with the elementary school. All these experiences gave me the realisation that my real interest lies in the interaction between people and the affect that art & design has on them.

After I graduated, I wasn’t able to find the study that gave me what I was looking for, so I decided to take a gap year. Since I love learning languages, travelling and exploring new places, I went to Spain for half a year.

I am currently a first year UX Design student at The Hague University of Applied Sciences, and looking for an internship starting in September 2020!
