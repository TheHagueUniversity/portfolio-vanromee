For this assignment, I made an interactive image that incorporates loops that create patterns. I made a speech bubble that says “Help me” inspired by how I’m feeling most of the time during this bootcamp. I attached this image to the mouse cursor so that everytime you move the cursor a trail of the speech bubbles will follow.
